FAQ

  • How did you make this website?

  • I coded it myself using Brackets. It was made using HTML and CSS languages. There's also a small bit of Javascript for this page for the form, but I'm not nearly as confident with it.

  • Would you be willing to help with Photoshop projects (editing photographs, removing glares, etc.)?

  • I would be intimidated, but I could certainly try! I'm not used to be asked to help with such things.

  • What mediums have you done before?

  • Digital, traditional, painting (watercolor, acrylic, paint), linocut, pottery, felting, charcoal, ink, oil pastel, collage, mixed media, photography, and probably more that I'm forgetting. My high school let us experiment with many different methods of getting creativity out. A lot of my branching out comes from there.

  • What do you plan to do with your education after college?

  • I don't know, to be honest. That's the big question on everyone's mind. My situation has led me to live my life day-by-day, since I never know when the next life-changing moment will occur. It sounds silly, but the future scares me. As such, I'm not sure I'm ready to make any hasty decisions yet on where to throw myself. I do plan on getting my bachelor's, but that's all I know.
