About Me

I've had a passion for all forms of art since I was a child. I used to play instruments, I've drawn in all sorts of mediums since I was young, and I've been a dancer for eleven years. It's been an amazing journey, and one I've learned much from.

Only recently did I start delving into my interests. My high school offered a few classes on Adobe products, the most notable being Photoshop. I took to the program immediately and have been using it since. A few years and several courses later, I've gathered a solid grasp of Photoshop and other Adobe products, namely Illustrator and InDesign.

Being able to make art in so many ways is impactful to me due to how my body is built. I am prone to injury, and thus, have been pulled away from expressing myself and my emotions in a physical manner many times. Turning to a pencil and paper in these times has helped me in more ways than one. Not only do I get to improve my skills, but I get to have fun doing it as well.

While I'm uncertain of what my future holds, I can only hope it's bright. I can see that I have potential to do great things with what I create. I just need to take the steps and reach for success.
